# Shiv Furniture ERP - Product Walkthrough Video Script

## Video Overview
**Title:** Shiv Furniture ERP - Complete System Walkthrough  
**Target Audience:** Stakeholders, Admins, Vendors, Customers  
**Total Duration:** ~5-7 Minutes  
**Tone:** Professional, Clear, and Informative

---

## 1. Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)

**Visual:**
- Landing Page of the Application.
- Logo animation of "Shiv Furniture".
- Split screen showing Admin Dashboard, Vendor Portal, and Customer Storefront.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Welcome to the official walkthrough of the Shiv Furniture Enterprise Resource Planning system. This comprehensive web application is designed to streamline operations, manage finances, and connect seamlessly with both vendors and customers."

"In this video, we will explore the three core pillars of our ecosystem:
1. **The Admin Suite** for complete business management.
2. **The Vendor Portal** for partner collaboration.
3. **The Customer Portal** for a premium shopping experience."

"Let's dive in."

---

## 2. Admin Module - The Command Center (0:45 - 2:30)

**Visual:**
- **Scene 1:** Login Screen. Admin enters credentials and logs in.
- **Scene 2:** **Admin Dashboard**. Mouse hovers over "Total Revenue", "Budget Utilization", and "Pending Orders" cards.
- **Scene 3:** Sidebar navigation scrolling to show modules.

**Audio:**
"We start with the Admin Module – the heart of the system. Upon logging in, the Admin is greeted by a dynamic **Dashboard**. This gives a real-time snapshot of the business's health, tracking Key Performance Indicators like Revenue, Expense Trends, and Budget Status."

**Visual:**
- **Scene 4:** Click on **"Master Data"** > **"Products"**. Show list of furniture items.
- **Scene 5:** Click on **"Budgets"**. Show the Budget vs. Actual comparison charts.
- **Scene 6:** Click on **"Transactions"** > **"Purchase Orders"**. Show the 'Create New PO' form.

**Audio:**
"The Admin has full control over **Master Data**, allowing management of Products, Service Costs, and Contacts. 

One of the standout features is **Budget Management**. The system allows granular control over cost centers and budgets, ensuring financial discipline. Admins can define budgets and track 'Actual vs. Projected' spending in real-time.

For operations, the **Transaction** module handles the complete lifecycle – from raising Purchase Orders to Vendors, to generating Sales Invoices for Customers. Everything is linked, ensuring data integrity."

---

## 3. Vendor Portal - Partner Collaboration (2:30 - 3:45)

**Visual:**
- **Scene 1:** Logout from Admin. Login as a **Vendor**.
- **Scene 2:** **Vendor Dashboard**. Highlight "Action Required" section showing Pending POs.
- **Scene 3:** Click on **"My Products"**. Show the vendor adding a new product (Cost, Category).

**Audio:**
"Next, we look at the **Vendor Portal**. We believe in empowering our partners. 

When a Vendor logs in, they see a personalized Dashboard. The **'Action Required'** section immediately notifies them of new Purchase Orders from Shiv Furniture that need approval."

"Vendors can also manage their own catalog using the **'My Products'** feature. They can list new items they supply, update prices, and categorize goods, which then update the master database for the Admin to review."

**Visual:**
- **Scene 4:** Click **"My Bills"** tab. Show list of submitted bills and their status (Paid/Due).
- **Scene 5:** Click **"Orders Received"**. Show details of a Purchase Order.

**Audio:**
"Financial transparency is key. Vendors can track all **Orders Received** and monitor the status of their **Bills**. They can see exactly which invoices are paid and which are pending, eliminating the need for constant follow-up emails."

---

## 4. Customer Portal - The Shopping Experience (3:45 - 5:00)

**Visual:**
- **Scene 1:** Login as a **Customer**.
- **Scene 2:** **Customer Dashboard**. Show "Outstanding Due" and "Recent Invoices".
- **Scene 3:** Click **"Place Order"** or **"Products"**.
- **Scene 4:** Browse the Product Grid (Glassmorphism UI). Add "Office Chair" to cart.
- **Scene 5:** Click **"Checkout"**. Show Cart Review and "Place Order" button.

**Audio:**
"Finally, the **Customer Portal**. We've designed a modern, e-commerce-style experience for our B2B and retail clients.

The Dashboard provides a clear financial summary—customers know exactly what they owe and what they've paid. 

To make a purchase, they browse the digital **Product Catalog**. With a sleek, user-friendly interface, they can add items to their cart and seamlessly **Checkout**. This generates a Sales Order in the system instantly."

**Visual:**
- **Scene 6:** Go to **"Invoices"** tab. Show a list of invoices. Hover over a "Pay Now" button.

**Audio:**
"Post-purchase, customers can view their **Order History** and download **Invoices**. The system separates Pending and Paid invoices, making account reconciliation effortless."

**Visual:**
- Split screen again showing all three roles active.
- Fade to "Shiv Furniture" Logo with contact details.

**Audio:**
"The Shiv Furniture ERP is more than just software; it's a complete ecosystem that synchronizes your internal team with your external partners.

Secure, Efficient, and Beautifully Designed.

Thank you for watching."
